About Rock Properties

Rock properties are fundamental to reservoir characterization and include:

  • Porosity: The void space in rock that can contain fluids
  • Water Saturation: The fraction of pore space occupied by water
  • Permeability: The ability of rock to transmit fluids
  • Formation Factor: Relates rock resistivity to formation water resistivity

These calculations are essential for reservoir evaluation, log analysis, and hydrocarbon resource assessment.
